सेवा परमो धर्म

Navigating through the whole page you must have got to know that ISKCON is a social group which helps people connect sprituality.You must have heard "seva paramo dharma", "service before self". This is what Sri Krishna says in srimadBhagavad Gita, serve and service are the good deeds which brings back the good deed , the cycle of karma.Iskcon provides various oppurtunnites and programmes to connect people to god viz; Sunday Open house, Cow potection,eco-villages,DONATE iskcon to feed poor-food for life,anna seva programme,seminar,retreat etc. which requires volunteers.
